About Me

Pushing the boundaries of 3D visualization

I'm a passionate and versatile creative with a strong foundation in 3D visualization, generative AI, and cutting-edge digital technologies. With hands-on experience in tools like Blender and CLO3D, I bring concepts to life through visually compelling animations and simulations. My journey blends creativity with technical expertise—ranging from AI model training and object detection systems to providing IT support in dynamic, real-world environments.

Driven by curiosity and a love for innovation, I constantly explore emerging tech to push the boundaries of what's possible in digital design and smart systems. Whether it's crafting immersive visuals or building intelligent solutions, I aim to create work that is both impactful and inspiring.

My Services

3D Modeling

Designing high-quality 3D models with a focus on precision and creativity. Expertise in creating props, and assets for games, films, and interactive media, ensuring optimized topology and attention to detail.

Environment Design

Crafting immersive and detailed 3D environments that tell stories and create atmospheric experiences.

PBR Texturing

Creating physically-based materials and textures that bring models to life with realistic surface properties, weathering effects, and material definition.

Logo Animations

Creating dynamic and visually appealing logo animations that enhance brand identity and leave a lasting impression. Expertise in motion graphics and animation techniques to bring logos to life with smooth transitions and creative effects.

AI model training

Developing and training custom AI models tailored for specific use cases, such as object detection and classification, optimized for deployment on edge devices with limited computational resources.
