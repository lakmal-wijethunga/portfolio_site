About Me

Pushing the boundaries of 3D visualization

I'm a passionate and versatile creative with a strong foundation in 3D visualization, generative AI, and cutting-edge digital technologies. With hands-on experience in tools like Blender and CLO3D, I bring concepts to life through visually compelling animations and simulations. My journey blends creativity with technical expertise—ranging from AI model training and object detection systems to providing IT support in dynamic, real-world environments.

Driven by curiosity and a love for innovation, I constantly explore emerging tech to push the boundaries of what's possible in digital design and smart systems. Whether it's crafting immersive visuals or building intelligent solutions, I aim to create work that is both impactful and inspiring.
